Tour Guide Robot (May 2025 - Present) ▶

Study investigates the effects of a mixed-agent team on user satisfaction and learning performance in robot-guided tour scenarios, and introduces a novel metric, "Following," based on observable human behaviors.

  • Hands-on experience with Toyota HSR in ROS Noetic framework.
  • ArUco marker detection with OpenCV 3.0 and PID control for head alignment.
  • Angle of rotation measurement to quantify "Following" behavior.
  • Human pose & facial expression tracking with OpenPose and MediaPipe.
  • User experiment design and data analysis.

Extracting Human Visual Perspective (Sept 2024 - May 2025) ▶

A multi-modal human VPT extraction algorithm using VSLAM and RGB-D data. It estimates gaze direction and head pose to understand human perspective in real time. Validated in simulated and real-world settings.

Sensor Fusion (Aug 2024 - April 2024) ▶

Member of Autonomous Robot Vehicle (ARV) team developing SLAM algorithms for fully autonomous vehicles in the Intelligent Ground Vehicle Competition. Responsible for refining the collision avoidance / path-planning algorithms, conducting simulations, and running tests in real-world scenarios to evaluate performance. Researching and implementing SLAM algorithms with Kalman Filters to reduce LiDAR mapping errors.
